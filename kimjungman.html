Kim Jung Man

I was tasked with creating a website for one of South Korea's prominent photographers. This website was used for La Nuit Blanche Paris.

Design: John Kim
Development: John Kim

The Challenge & Approach

Kim Jung Man, a traditional photographer, was hesitant about having a website. Despite his concerns, there was a demand, especially with an upcoming showcase in Paris. In response to requests for information about his works, he opted for a practical approach, sending out PDFs upon inquiry.

I took a straightforward approach to address this. I designed a website that mimicked a PDF reader, allowing Kim Jung Man to showcase his work in a format similar to what he would typically send in his PDFs. The simplicity of the website resonated with him, providing both satisfaction and the desired online presence.
