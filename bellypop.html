Bellypop

Bellypop approached me seeking a dynamic motion graphics logo that resonates with their vibrant and playful brand targeted at kids. Their vision is to create something visually captivating to be featured across their social media platforms.

Art Direction: John Kim
Motion Graphics: John Kim
Photography: Jayce

The Challenege & Approach

Initially faced with the challenge of undecided brand colors, I took the creative approach of incorporating vibrant colors that retract to the original black logo. This decision aimed to capture the playfulness of Bellypop's brand, creating a visually appealing and engaging motion graphics logo for their social media presence.

Bellypop Socials

Bellypop presented me with a collection of video footage lacking a clear direction. Their request was for me to create a short compelling promotional sequence for their social media platforms. The objective was to show the significance of water consumption, showing how kids can have both enjoyment and health benefits from this essential habit.
