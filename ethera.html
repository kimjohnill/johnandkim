Ethera

Collaborating directly with the CEO to facilitate Ethera's transition into a scheduling app catering to all therapists, not just exclusive to its members.

UXUI Design: John Kim

Background

Founded in 2020, Ethera is a company that offers office spaces for therapists without their own. Providing a shared office environment and an app for scheduling and various tasks exclusive to Ethera members. Now, they've made a significant decision to open up access to this scheduling app to a wider audience.

Role

Even though the product was well-established, I was brought in to address some specific concerns. Initially, employers had difficulty efficiently managing their employees via the app, lacking a user-friendly method for supervisors to oversee employee accounts. Secondly, for couples receiving therapy together, there was an issue when one decided to attend individually. All communication occurred within the application, yet when a couple registered, there was no way to separately message an individual from the partnered account.
