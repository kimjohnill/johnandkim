Suitman

Suitman, an artist capturing images from across the globe, required a website to showcase his extensive collection.

Design: John Kim
Development: John Kim

The Challenge & Approach

Initially overwhelmed with a large volume of images, I found inspiration in Suitman's public artworks. Following his method, I decided to showcase them similarly, creating a wall of images on a single page. This design choice effectively showed the immense scale of his project.
